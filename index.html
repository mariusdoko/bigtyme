About BigTyme

Big Tyme Billiards is Houston’s favorite pool hall -- a place where you can unwind with old friends and make new ones. Enjoy a cold beer or a refreshing cocktail and play a round of pool on some of the best pool tables in the area. Ask us about our daily drink specials, and if you’re looking to take your game to the next level, join one of our weekly pool leagues. Whether it’s your first time or you’re a regular, you can always count on being greeted with friendly service, a laid-back atmosphere, and fun people guaranteed to make any night of the week an unforgettable one.
